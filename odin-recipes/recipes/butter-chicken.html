Butter Chicken

Description

Butter chicken is a North Indian dish created in the 1950s in the Moti Mahal, New Delhi when a chef decided to stir the leftover tandoori chicken in a rich buttery tomato gravy. Recipe from Cafe Delites

Ingredients

For the chicken,

  • Boneless, skinless chicken 800 g
  • Greek yoghurt 1/2 cup
  • Minced garlic 3/2 tbsp
  • Minced ginger 1 tbsp
  • Salt 1 tsp
  • Ground cumin 1 tsp
  • Ground turmeric 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder 1 tsp

For the sauce,

  • Crushed tomatoes 400 g
  • White onions 300 g
  • Heavy cream 300 g
  • Olive oil 250 g
  • Ghee 2 tbsp
  • Minced garlic 3/2 tbsp
  • Minced ginger 1 tbsp
  • Sugar 1 tbsp
  • Garam masala 3/2 tsp
  • Ground cumin 3/2 tsp
  • Ground coriander 3/2 tsp
  • Ground turmeric 3/2 tsp
  • Kasoori methi 1 tsp
  • Red chili powder 3/2 tsp
  • Salt To taste
